Computer Floors

Raised Access flooring systems provide easy access and routing for cabling, ventilation ductwork, and more.

We have carried out the installation and adaptation of many computer floors. We recommend steel-pedestal systems as they offer excellent performance and durability, and are available with short lead-in periods. The component parts that we utilise are all high grade, and are offered at competitive rates. We can offer a design and specification service for any project.

Access floors are widely used in all types of locations, but more frequently in large open plan office spaces, computer facilities or communication/internet based companies.
